Ingredients

    2 individual tea bags
    1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
    1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
    1 cup water
    1 cup milk
    1/4 cup packed brown sugar
    2 tablespoons refrigerated French vanilla nondairy creamer
    Whipped topping and ground nutmeg, optional


Directions

    Place the tea bags, cinnamon, ginger and allspice in the coffee 
filter of a drip coffeemaker. Add water; brew according to manufacturer's directions.
    Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, combine the milk, brown sugar and creamer. 
Cook and stir over medium heat until heated through and sugar is dissolved. 
Pour milk mixture into mugs; stir in tea. Dollop with whipped topping and sprinkle 
with nutmeg if desired. Yield: 2 servings.
